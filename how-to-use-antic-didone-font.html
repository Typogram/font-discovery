About Antic Didone

Antic Didone is a high-contrast serif font from the Didone classification, known for its sharp, delicate serifs and striking vertical stress. Based on the original Antic font structure, Antic Didone has "Didone" influence and takes stylistic cues from the renowned Didot family of type designers, like Firmin Didot who is credited for establishing use of "Didone" or high contrast modern serifs. Its elegant structure makes it a favorite for projects where drama and style are front and center.
