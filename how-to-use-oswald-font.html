All About Oswald

Oswald draws inspiration from gothic sans-serif typefaces of the late 19th century. Its condensed form and sharp lines make it a space-efficient choice for headlines and banners. The font's strong, industrial character lends itself well to editorial design and advertising where boldness is key.Â  Its bold, condensed style makes it effective for conveying strength and authority.Â  With its sharp lines, Oswald excels in headlines, banners, and impactful text.

How to Use Oswald for Logo?

Oswald is a condensed sans-serif that offers a tall and structured look. It works well for logos that need to convey stability, authority, or boldness, making it a great choice for media, sports, or corporate branding. It pairs well with sharp, clean lines and simple iconography for a powerful and assertive logo.
