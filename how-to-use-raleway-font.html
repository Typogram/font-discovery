Font of the Week 

All About Raleway

Raleway is a stylish sans serif known for its thin strokes, inspired by neo-grotesque sans serifs. It exudes elegance and sophistication, perfect for larger headings, titles, and modern, creative design work. Its sleek lines and light weight make it ideal for a luxurious, minimalist aesthetic in large display sizes. My favorite part? Raleway has multiple weights and styles to support the design for a large, complex project. 

Raleway Font Details:

  • Elegant and Graceful: Raleway’s thin strokes give off a sleek and sophisticated feel. 
  • High-Contrast: It features high-contrast strokes, particularly in its bold weights, creating an upscale look.
  • Wide Range of Styles: Raleway offers multiple weights and styles for diverse design needs
  • Lining and Old Style Figures perfect for all type of lists and numerical figures

How to Use Raleway for Logo ?

Raleway is an elegant and sophisticated font, characterized by its thin, sleek strokes and modern appeal. It’s perfect for luxury brands, high-end products, or fashion-related logos. Use its thin versions for delicate, refined logos, or the bold versions for more impactful designs, pairing it with elegant visuals.

How to Use Raleway for Branding & Marketing?

Raleway’s elegant and sophisticated style makes it an ideal font for luxury branding. It is perfect for high-end product packaging, upscale magazine ads, and premium web designs. Its sleek lines create a sense of exclusivity and refinement that resonates with affluent audiences. There is also a nice bonus –Raleway comes with a dotted version that has dots as strokes. Using Raleway dots with Raleway quickly adds a quirky tone with beautiful visual texture. 

Raleway Font Pairing

  • Raleway + Roboto
  • Raleway + Lora
  • Raleway + Merriweather
