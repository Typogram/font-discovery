Behind the Typeface

Originally designed by Les Usherwood, Alexon was later expanded and digitized by Steve Jackaman in 1992. Its roots trace back to Basilea, a 1965 award-winning design by Markus J. Low. While Basilea brought a modern sharpness to the serif category, Alexon continues that legacy with subtle strength and composure.
