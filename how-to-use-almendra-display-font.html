How to Use Almendra Display: A Gothic Calligraphy Typeface Packed with Modern Details

If you enjoyed this post, subscribe to get updates from our blog!

Font of the Week

About Almendra Display

Almendra Display, part of the Almendra font family, is a Gothic calligraphy typeface that effortlessly blends historical charm with modern flair. Inspired by the rich traditions of calligraphy, its flourishes and intricate split serifs add an elegant, sophisticated touch to any design project. Perfect for making a bold statement, Almendra Display captures the essence of timeless craftsmanship with contemporary appeal.

Almendra Display Type Details

Characterized by its thin strokes, delicate forms with split serifs, Almendra Display is best suited for display purposes where elegant character and traditional personality are key. This font shines in headlines, titles, and any graphic design work that requires an updated ornamental touch with a historical flair.

How to use Almendra Display for Logo

Almendra's thin strokes, decorative embellishments, and delicate details can break down in small sizes. Due to the intricacy of its design, it's best to use this font as a supporting display typeface for marketing as part of the brand identity.  

How to use Almendra Display for Branding & Marketing

Almendra Display is perfect for branding projects aiming to blend boldness with gothic, medieval or vintage sophistication. Use it for titles in marketing materials to evoke a sense of tradition fused with modern flair. Its distinctive character adds depth to product packaging, enhancing the overall narrative of a brand. Almendra Display is part of the Almendra font family, which also includes a standard version and a small cap variation.

Almendra Display Font Pairings

Pairing Almendra Display with simpler fonts can bring harmony to a design project. Here are three pairings:

  • Montserrat: A versatile sans-serif that contrasts beautifully with Almendra Display’s ornate details, ideal for body text in graphic design.
  • Roboto: This modern sans-serif provides clarity, balancing the historical style of Almendra Display in logo design.
  • Open Sans: Its readability and neutral style make it an excellent pairing for Almendra Display in marketing materials, allowing the graphics to shine.
